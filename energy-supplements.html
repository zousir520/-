4. 科学使用方法：避免陷阱，安全提升能量

4. Science-Based Usage: Avoiding Pitfalls for Safe Energy Boost

安全使用指南
Safe Usage Guidelines

4.1 正确时机，正确人群

4.1 Right Timing, Right People

Healthy individuals don't need supplements—overusing fructose may cause osmotic diarrhea. Use only under medical guidance for confirmed metabolic issues (e.g., chronic fatigue, post-surgical malnutrition).

4.2 正确给药

4.2 Proper Administration

  • IV formulations (e.g., lipid emulsions) require controlled infusion (<1.5ml/kg/h) to avoid hyperlipidemia.
  • Oral supplements should be taken with meals (fat-soluble vitamins pair with fats for better absorption). Never use insulin-containing cocktails on an empty stomach to prevent hypoglycemia.

4.3 监测与禁忌症

4.3 Monitoring & Contraindications

Regularly check blood sugar, lipids, and organ function (e.g., triglycerides every 2 weeks for long-term lipid emulsion users). Avoid:

  • Hyperlipidemia patients (lipid emulsions contraindicated)
  • Fructose intolerance (avoid fructose-based products)
  • Allergic individuals (screen for ingredient sensitivities first)
